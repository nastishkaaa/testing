About the scholarship

This scholarship programme is designed for international students who plan to study in Slovakia in selected study fields. The goal is to support talented students and strengthen international cooperation in education, science, technology and innovation.

Students can choose a public Slovak higher education institution and apply for a selected study programme. The scholarship can help cover living costs during studies.

Important notice: Applicants are responsible for preparing all required documents on time, including proof of identity, CV, motivation letter and admission documents.
Registration opening date will be published later. Please check this website regularly.

Basic eligibility requirements

  • Applicant must be a citizen of a country other than Slovakia.
  • Applicant must apply for a full-time study programme.
  • Applicant must prepare a CV and motivation letter in English.
  • Applicant must select a supported study programme.
  • Applicant must provide true and complete information.

Exceptions cannot be granted for basic requirements. The application may be checked by the ministry and by the selected higher education institution.

Frequently asked questions

Who can apply for the scholarship?
International applicants who want to study selected full-time programmes in Slovakia may apply.
How much money can I receive?
The scholarship may provide up to €500 per month during the supported period.
Do I need a motivation letter?
Yes, applicants usually need to submit a motivation letter in English.
Can I apply for doctoral studies?
This demo programme does not support doctoral studies.
